Business Plan

The short version.

Pix and Grid is a small studio that replaces slow agency timelines and beige templates with flat-rate, color-driven websites — built fast, priced clearly, and designed to convert.

Mission

Bold sites, no six-week wait.

Give founders and small teams a website that looks like it cost five figures, without the five-figure timeline or the hourly-rate anxiety.

Market

Startups & small business

Solo founders, local businesses, and 2–20 person teams who need a credible web presence fast and don't have an in-house design team.

Revenue Model

Flat-rate packages + care plans

Three fixed-price product tiers cover build cost; recurring monthly care plans and add-on sprints cover ongoing revenue.
